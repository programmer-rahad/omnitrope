Omnitrope® is available in sterile, ready-to-use cartridges of 5 mg/1.5 mL, 10 mg/1.5 mL or 15 mg/1.5 mL. The SurePal® 5, SurePal® 10 and SurePal® 15 are designed to deliver consistent doses, providing assurance for you and convenience for your patient. The pre-diluted, multiple-use cartridge means quick injection preparation, and the dose knob and cartridge window will help ensure consistent dose delivery every time.

Teaching your patients to properly administer Omnitrope® using the SurePal® pen can be easy with step-by-step guides in both printable and video formats. Just ask them to visit www.Omnitrope.ca and log in with their DIN.
